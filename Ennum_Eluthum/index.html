Every person dreams to become what they aspire to be, until bogged down by various setbacks, thus depriving not just themselves and also the society inclusively.

In India, education is measured in terms of literacy and not as the tool to enable a person to acquire their best potentials that make them resourceful capitals of tomorrow's India.

This is where we step in, trying our possible means to meet both the ends so as to enable the deprived lot to march towards their dream, we being a little light through their walk.

# WHO WE ARE?

We are a small bunch that aims to help the young curious minds to orchestrate self development with the use of pertinent means, who are indispensable part of promising future for our society and the nation altogether.

We are trying to help and fund on educational institutions who are struggling to meet their basic amenities and provide scholarships for young ignited minds for their new scholastic venture so as to pursue their dreams.

We try to provide some handouts for small development projects that support and enable people to meet their basic needs and convenience.

REQUEST AND SUGGESTION

#MISSION

To serve as a means to aspiring but economically deprived young minds in successfully finishing their academics.

To fund on projects on schools that lack proper functional construction like sanitation, drinking water plant, and other essential needs.

To provide Social Development Projects for students by conducting workshops and awareness sessions on Physical Health, Hygiene and Mental Health.

Also envisage to preserving the traditional and cultural art forms of our country.

To provide skill enhancement to youth by exposing them to training and workshops on the based of their intrinsic potential.

To provide guidance /counseling by experts on career and skill development.

To lend a helping hand to the needy & supportless people to meet their basic needs like health, food, clothing, shelter, etc.

#VISION

To provide a safe and healthy platform for underprivileged individuals to carve themselves to their best potential economically, socially, physically and mentally to be better persons and thus making way for a better tomorrow by their valuable growth, so as to be fruitful contributors for our society.

#SERVICE & PROJECTS

The proposed services of the trust are categorized as below,

EDUCATION
Scholarship, Innovation Project Funding, Workshops & Crash Courses, Smart Classrooms, Providing Essentials.
INFRASTRUCTURE
Funds for Construction & Renovation of Schools to Ensure a Safe & Secure Environment.
SOCIAL
Providing Basic Amenities like Food, Water etc. Books for Library, Welfare Activities.
HEALTH & SANITATION
Health Camps, Awareness Activities.
