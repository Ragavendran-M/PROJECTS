CELEBRATORY

#CELEBRATORY

This trust was seeded in memory of our beloved grandparents Sri. S.CHELLIAH (1912 -1969) & Smt.PAPPAMMAL (1922-2018) and their affectionate eldest son Sri. V.C. GANESAN (1947-2020).

Mrs. Pappammal Chelliah, mother of seven children who was destined to be a single parent since 1969, despite hailing from a lower middle-class background. With her dogged determination and virtue, she pushed them above the brim with the help of their eldest son and his family, who always continue to guide us benevolently. They understood the power of education and made all possible means to equip not just their children but also have been a beacon of hope to many. With their perseverance, they kept working for students upliftment despite all their hardships. Thanks to them, we all are now renowned, respected and responsible citizens.

This trust is in obeisance to our grandparents, their eldest son, and the most admired missile man, our beloved Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Sir's vision for making better tomorrows for India via youngsters. We are setting steps to help and guide the potential yet deprived young dreamers, providing them a platform to learn and nourish.
